Hamburger Recipe

Description

The most popular meal in the entire world!

The delicious special hamburger, the old school one, without weird things

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bread
  • Sauces of choice
  • Hamburger beef
  • Slice of cheese
  • Slice of ham
  • Lettuce
  • Slice of tomato
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Cook the hamburger beef 5 minutes each side.
  2. Fry the egg for 3 minutes.
  3. Add choice sauce to the bread.
  4. Put the lettuce and the tomato in the base bread.
  5. Put the beef.
  6. Put the cheese and the ham
  7. Wrap it all with the top bread.
  8. Eat it, eat it, eat it!
