Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

The most italian plate you will ever see!

This is the meal all our grandmas love to make us! And for that, we all love our grandmas

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Onion
  • Pimento
  • Ground meat

Steps

  1. Chop the onion and the pimento.
  2. Cook both with the ground meat.
  3. Add the bolognese sauce.
  4. Condiment.
  5. In another put, boil some saltwater and cook the spaghetti for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve on a plate the bolognese sauce in top of the spaghetti.
  7. Launch time!
