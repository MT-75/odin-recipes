Pizza Recipe

Description

The food that everyone loves, the good old pizza. Easy to make, but difficult to master, creating this delicacy is within your reach.

This time we are going to prepare the most basic version of this dish: a round dough as a base, a tomato sauce in the middle and the delicious creamy cheese on top of everything.

Ingredients

  • 200g of floor
  • 140ml of water
  • 2 tea spoon of salt
  • 1 spoon of Oil
  • 1/2 tea spoon of sugar
  • 150ml of tomato sauce
  • 200g of cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the floor, water, salt, oil and sugar, mix the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Shape the dough in a circular recipient.
  3. Add the tomato sauce on it.
  4. Put the recipient in the oven at 180 grades for 20 minutes.
  5. Cut the cheese and add it on top
  6. Oven it for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
